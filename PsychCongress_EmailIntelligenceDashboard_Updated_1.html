🔬 Segment-Specific Prescriptions
💎 BOFU — Bottom of Funnel (Highest Value)
▼

Why they're your #1 asset: BOFU audiences consistently deliver 40–60% open rates across Elevate, NPI, and PC Regionals — 2–3x your full-list average. Every missed send to BOFU is revenue left on the table.

✅ Elevate BOFU Rate Day-Of: 59.89% open · NPI Women's Mental Health BOFU: 50.68% · NPI KBYG Registrants: 41.88%

📌 Prescription: Send BOFU 72 hrs before ANY rate change. Include specific dollar savings ("Save $200 before midnight"). Add a P.S. reinforcing the deadline. Never send generic content to BOFU — they know the event.

🎓 Registrants — Most Engaged, Most Under-Served
▼

The surprise segment: Registrants deliver your highest click rates — NPI Reg KBYG Reminder: 18.3% click. Elevate Scholarship Accepted: 40% click rate. These people are committed and want logistics, content, and validation.

✅ PAI Post-Event Survey: 57.3% open · 35.4% click · PCR Day 1 Morning Flash: 60.9% open · 37.5% click

📌 Prescription: Build a mandatory Registrant Welcome Sequence: Day 0 (confirmation + hotel), Day 7 (Career Fair), Day 30 (session planning), Day 60 (KBYG preview), Day 7 before (KBYG full), Day 3 (logistics), Day 0 (welcome). 8 touchpoints minimum.

📧 TOFU — Top of Funnel (Volume Driver)
▼

The awareness engine: TOFU sends (60K–245K+ for PC2026) average 7–15% opens. Faculty personal letters dramatically outperform generic promo copy (Brittany Albright Letter: 15.4% vs 8–9% average).

✅ Send faculty personal letters to TOFU — not registration CTAs. The clinician speaking to the clinician pulls conversion to MOFU.

📌 Prescription: Reserve hard-sell CTAs for MOFU/BOFU. Test "A note from Dr. [Name]" subject lines vs. event-feature subjects. Single CTA: faculty bio or session detail page.

🏆 Alumni — Hidden Revenue Source
▼

Massively underutilized: Alumni open at 8–22%, with NPI Alumni Last Chance hitting 21.9%. Past attendees are statistically most likely to buy again. Yet the USPC 2026 reg list is only 1,217 — a fraction of alumni potential.

✅ NPI Alumni Last Chance: 21.9% open · Elevate Scholarship Alumni: 15.4% open

📌 Prescription: Launch "Welcome Back" alumni sequence 4 months pre-event. Use: exclusive early-bird "Alumni Rate," co-chair personal letter, prior-year highlights, referral incentive. Goal: 25–35% alumni conversion before opening to cold list.

🏆 The BOFU-First Rule
1
BOFU Before Full List — Always
Send rate-deadline emails to BOFU 24–72 hrs before the full list sees them. They convert at 2–5x the rate. Give them first access to savings — they feel special, you get better ROI.
2
Never Send BOFU Generic Content
BOFU contacts know you. Subject lines must contain a dollar amount, deadline, or clinical specificity. "Final 48 hours — save $150" beats "Don't miss PC 2026." Every time.
3
The Day-Of Email Is Non-Negotiable
Elevate BOFU Day-Of: 59.89% open rate. That is your single best email asset. Send it every campaign, every rate deadline. No exceptions. It's your highest ROI send.
📬 Sequence Architecture
✅ Registrant Welcome Sequence (8 Emails)
Confirmation → Hotel → Career Fair → KBYG Preview → KBYG Full → Logistics → Day-Before → Day-Of Welcome. Currently missing at full scale — building it is your #1 post-registration lever.
🔄 14-Day Follow-Up Rule
A second contact 14 days after any rate email pulls 50% of the original response at marginal cost. You're already doing T-7 and Day-Of — add T+14 (value-add content for non-openers) to every campaign.
🎯 Engagement-Based Re-Segmentation
After each send, move openers to MOFU, clickers to BOFU. Non-openers after 3 sends → "We miss you" re-engagement with a completely different subject line approach. Don't keep sending to non-engagers.
✍️ Copy Formula — What Works
✅ Faculty-First Subject Lines for TOFU
"A note from Dr. Mattingly" outperforms "PC 2026 Updates." Clinicians trust clinicians. The personal letter format (Brittany Albright: 15.4% OR, Stephanie Kress: 15.2%) consistently beats generic promo.
✅ Clinical Specificity in Body Copy
"ADHD + mood comorbidity" beats "mental health topics." Clinicians filter for relevance instantly. Your best emails name specific conditions, medications, and patient scenarios.
❌ Avoid Subject Line Fatigue
"Last Chance" works once per campaign. After that, rotate to clinical hooks: "What's changing in TRD treatment — New Orleans 2026" pulls fresher opens than "Don't miss your chance."
📈 Growth Levers — Untapped Opportunities
🚀 Cross-Sell Elevate → PC2026
1,037+ Elevate 2026 registrants are warm leads for PC2026. Post-Elevate email within 48 hours of closing. Offer exclusive Elevate-to-PC discount code. Expected: 8–15% conversion.
🚀 PCR → PC2026 Pipeline
PCR registrants (free CE, online) are TOFU for in-person events. After each PCR session, send: "Liked today's session? 40+ more in New Orleans." 200+ warm contacts per session.
🚀 Post-Event On-Demand Nurture
NPI on-demand emails (25.9% and 16.4% OR) show strong engagement. Build for every event: Access notification → Content reminder → CE deadline → Next event preview.
🚀 Alumni Referral Program
NPI Alumni Referral emails prove the model. Build a portfolio-wide referral program: attend + bring a colleague = discount for both. Group discount emails (Elevate Dec 2025: 11.7% OR) show concept resonates.
🧪 A/B Testing Priorities
🔬 Test #1: From Name
"Dr. Greg Mattingly" vs. "Psych Congress" as From Name for TOFU sends. Faculty-named sends likely lift open rate 15–25% based on your letter email data.
🔬 Test #2: Subject — Clinical Hook vs. FOMO
"What's changing in TRD treatment — New Orleans 2026" vs. "3,500 clinicians will be there. Will you?" Both valid — test to your MOFU list and track CTR, not just OR.
🔬 Test #3: Send Day for BOFU
Your BOFU sends skew Tue/Thu. Test Wednesday 8:00 AM — often a dead day that stands out in clinician inboxes precisely because it's unexpected.
🔬 Test #4: P.S. Line
Add a P.S. to every BOFU email: "P.S. — Registration closes at midnight. After that, rates increase by $[X]." The P.S. is the 2nd most-read element in any email. Never skip it.
⚕️ Compliance & Best Practices
⚕️ Audience: Doctors, Psychiatrists, DOs, PAs, NPs, Mental Health Clinicians. Every email must answer: "What will I learn that helps my patients?" Generic event promotion is discarded immediately.
🔒 No medical claims in subject lines. CME/CE language must be accurate. Avoid superlatives ("best," "cure") in clinical contexts. All faculty credentials must be verifiable. Testimonials must be attributed accurately.
📋 CAN-SPAM / GDPR compliance. All emails include unsubscribe. Physical address in footer. Suppress bounces and unsubscribes within 10 business days. Segment by consent type for EU contacts.
🎓 CE/CME accuracy. Always state exact credit hours. Credit type (AMA PRA Category 1, ANCC, AAPA) must be correct per audience. PA Institute: AAPA-approved language. NP Institute: ANCC accreditation where applicable.
